About This Game

The Grid is a virtual world. Players around the world enter into the Grid to participate the Tachyon Games, a prestigious survival competition where they fight for the glory of their clans. The Grid simulates an alternate reality that with is filled with hostile and dangerous scenarios to challenge and test the participants.

The Cloud is a group of thinkers. Their ability to think outside the box allows them to alter the artificial laws of nature in the Grid to their own benefits. Controlling the elements is common to the members of the Cloud Clan. This special ability allows them to go through natural disasters easily and thus gives them advantage in the simulations.

Members of the mountain clan are firm and strong. They possess very good stamina and they have rock solid survival skills. Already familiar with ways to survive, the Mountain Clan is the fastest to settle in the hostile environment around them. This advantage gives the Mountain Clan more opportunities to inspect their surroundings.

The members of the Moon Clan are known to be both agile and smart. Their ability is to observe and learn. They are able to learn the ability of other clans and use it in the right situation. This adjusting ability allow them to shine the brightest in the most difficult situation.

The Sun prides itself as being the bravest of all clans. Its members are known to be high spirited and have no fear of death. They are ambitious and strong willed people, eager to win the game. If anyone is excited to go for the simulation, it would be The Sun Clan. With their determination, the Sun Clan is the strongest in a one-on-one battle.

Just like the River, members of this clan are resolute and united towards the same goal. Even without a strong clan structure, they are spontaneous and able to adapt to their situations quickly. They don’t like to spend too much time spent on planning or strategizing. Its members in the real world are mostly people who have fast reflexes and strong team players. This clan is especially strong when facing surprise attacks.
